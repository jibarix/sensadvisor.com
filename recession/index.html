Short-term vs Medium-term View Multi-horizon overlay + historical warning behavior

Why it matters: This view separates medium-term vulnerability from near-term immediacy.

How to read: The system is estimated at multiple horizons: 12 months (watch), 6 months (alert), and 3 months (alarm). It is normal to see medium-term risk rise before short-term risk does.

What to do with it: If the short-term (3–6 month) lines rise meaningfully, that suggests conditions are shifting toward more immediate risk. If only the 12-month line is elevated, it can indicate vulnerability without imminence.
